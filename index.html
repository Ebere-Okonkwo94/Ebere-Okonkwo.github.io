Who Am I?

An Amabssador for Mental Health Awareness

What I Represent

I stand to bridge the divide in mental health awareness in Nigeria. People, both men and women deserve to know that they are worth more than some uninformed individuals has had them believe; what unfortunate circumstances, and even their unbelieving minds have made them subscribe to.

Not many actually reach out for help in conversations regarding their mental health because they do not want to be judged by the society. There appears to be no sympathy for such causes. Such people do not want to be seen as nut cases, so keep to themselves even if it translates to them dying in silence.

I represent the channel that brings this seemingly far-fetched hope to these people, and helping them through different stages of depression. I am a voice that screams out to as many that would listen, if albeit covertly; "It is ok to not be ok". I am your mental health social worker.
